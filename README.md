## Project Overview  
This repository contains my digital portfolio for the CSE0001 Digital Literacy course. Acting in the role of a Student Digital Ambassador, I have completed five distinct modules to demonstrate foundational digital skills. This repository serves as a permanent showcase of my ability to navigate the digital world safely, communicate professionally, and utilize modern collaboration and technical tools.

## Module Summaries  

* **Module 1: Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic ** A visual resource created to explain the core concepts of digital literacy, essential digital tools for software and logic testing, and safe internet practices. Located in `task-1-presentation/`.

* **Module 2: Build Your Student Digital Portfolio ** The establishment of a professional digital footprint across GitHub, Kaggle, and ResearchGate to showcase my technical projects (such as Python algorithms) and academic progression. Located in `task-2-portfolio/`.

* **Module 3: Explore Coding & Collaboration Platforms ** Documentation of foundational logic testing using HackerRank's Python track, alongside the creation of a cloud-based collaboration tool using Google Workspace. Located in `task-3-platforms/`.

* **Module 4: Draft a Professional Email & Etiquette Guide ** Drafts of professional academic and corporate internship emails, paired with a quick-reference Do's and Don'ts checklist for responsible online behavior. Located in `task-4-email-etiquette/`.

* **Module 5: Cybercrime Awareness Case Study & Prevention Guide ** A detailed case study analyzing UPI payment request fraud targeting college students, accompanied by an actionable 8-point cybercrime prevention checklist. Located in `task-5-cybercrime/`.
